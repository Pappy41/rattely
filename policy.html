Business User Privacy Policy

By creating a business profile on RATTELY you accept these guidelines for processing personal data on the Website. (Date: 26-10-2020).

1. COLLECTING INFORMATION ON THE WEBSITE
1.1 COMPANY DATA

When you create a business profile on the Website, we collect the following master data: Your name, company address, domain, company name, country, password and your email address. The purpose of the collection of this information is to identify you as a customer when you log on to the Website. Furthermore, you can choose to add more information to your business profile, such as your details, website and company information. If you are a customer of RATTELY, we also collect information relevant to the fulfilment of the agreement between you and us.
